Welcome to the Virtual Solar System

This illustration simplifies the functioning of our Solar System. Depicted below are 9 planets orbiting the Sun. To provide a relatable perspective, their speeds have been equated to Earth's orbital period, where 365.26 days are condensed into 1 second. Notably, when translated into this timeframe, the velocities of Mercury and Venus become so rapid that human eyes struggle to perceive their motion. To enhance visibility, a minor adjustment has been made by slowing down the speed of each planet using a 5-second interval.
