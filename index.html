Welcome to the Virtual Solar System

This is the simple illustration of how our Solar Systems works. In below illustration there are 9 planets revolving around the sun. There speeds are compared with Earth Revolutional Period and converted into seconds i.e., 365.26 days = 1s. In the following illustration when converted to seconds the speed of mercury,venus was so fast that our eyes couldn't catch its speed. In order to make little slower 5s has been added to each planets speeds.
